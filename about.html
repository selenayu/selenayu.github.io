About This Site

What is the first animal that comes to your mind when you hear the term "Good Pets"? Is it a dog? A cat? Or maybe a hamster? Whatever your answer may be now, it is my goal today to convince you that just like cats and dogs, birds too, can be great companions too!
(Perhaps even better than dogs and cats)

More often than not, I see people dismissing the possibility of birds being potential companions. To those people, birds are loud, obnixous, and maybe even dangerous (just look at their beaks *gasp*).

By making this website, it is my goal to show those who feel indifference or fear towards birds that, just like puppies and kitties, birds can become amazing, intelligent, loving pets.
